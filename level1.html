Help

The game begins with a main puzzle screen showing the scrambled images. Simply slide one or more puzzle pieces into the empty space to rearrange the pieces into the complete the puzzle. The user is provided with a single space at first. While playing, if you are lost trying to solve the puzzle you can get a hint of what the completed image is. The next setting is the difficulty of the puzzle. TO begin the user chooses the difficulty level they would like to play at. The more rows and columns, the harder the puzzle will be to complete.
